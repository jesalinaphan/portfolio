How it started

This project was created for ENGN0032, a design based engineering class. With an emphasis on utilizing CAD, the prompt was to create any chair that we would want to have. Who wouldn't want a chair that brings the full movie theater experience to your home?

Since the prompt didn't specify a certain type of chair, my initial brainstorm included many different categories of chairs. Here were some of my early ideas.

Personas and Design Ideas

After deciding I wanted to move forward on creating the Ultimate Movie Chair, I started thinking a lot about what type of person would enjoy this chair. I created a persona map to help with this process and then started sketching out possible ideas for the structure of the chair.
Elements I wanted to include:
- comfy material/cushioning
- storage for food
- connected TV or projector

technology research

Since the chair had a lot of technological elements, I did a little bit of research regarding the feasability of each element and here were my results.

CAD Modeling + 3D Printed Prototype

After deciding on the final design, I turned to Fusion360 to create a CAD model of the chair, which I then 3D printed as my 1/12 scale final prototype.
